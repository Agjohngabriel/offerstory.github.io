Quick FAQs

Shop smarter, save more.

How does Offer Story work?
Offer Story is a local marketplace app that allows businesses to showcase their products through visually engaging stories. Users can discover nearby stores and products based on their location. Simply open the app, browse through stories, and find what you need.
Is Offer Story free to use?
Yes, Offer Story is completely free to download and use for both users and businesses.
How can I become a store owner on Offer Story?
To become a store owner on Offer Story, simply download the app and follow the on-boarding process. You'll need to provide basic business information and create engaging product stories.
What kind of products can I find on Offer Story?
Offer Story offers a wide range of products from various categories, including fashion, electronics, home goods, food, and more. You can use the search and filter options to find specific items.
How can I contact a store for more information?
Each store profile on Offer Story includes contact information, such as phone number and address. You can directly reach out to the store through the provided details.
